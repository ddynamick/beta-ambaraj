Who We Are

Ahmedabad Barsakh Rajput Gnati Panch

The Ahmedabad Barsakh Rajput Gnati Panch is the governing body of the Barsakh Rajput community based in Ahmedabad, with roots going back to Samvat 1296. We are committed to preserving the heritage, traditions and values of our proud Rajput community.

Our organisation works to strengthen the bonds between community members, celebrate our shared cultural heritage, support the education and wellbeing of members, and ensure the continuation of our traditions for future generations.

The Panch organises regular events including our annual Diwali function, sports competitions, cultural programmes and community welfare initiatives throughout the year.

Community First

We place the welfare of our community members above all else.

Education

Supporting the academic pursuits of our young generation.

Heritage

Preserving the rich history and traditions of Barsakh Rajputs.

Unity

Bringing all 12 Rajput Shakhas together under one banner.

Our Mission

What We Stand For

Education Support

Providing scholarships and recognising academic achievements of our community's students at all levels.

Sports & Culture

Organising annual sports events and cultural programmes to keep our community spirit alive and thriving.

Record Keeping

Maintaining Vanshavali (genealogy) records of 1000+ community families through the Ankleswariya Barot tradition.

Matrimonial Help

Facilitating Vivah and Vevishal within the community with traditional protocols and modern directory support.

Our History

Origin Story

બારશાખ રાજપૂત – The Beginning

સંવત ૧૨૯૬ ની માગશર સુદ ૧૦ ને દિવસે — ૭૭૮ વર્ષ પહેલા — સિધ્ધપુર મુકામે રાજ્પૂત જ્ઞાતિ નો સમૂહ-મેળો ભરાયેલ. તે વખતે છત્રીસ પ્રકારના (શાખ) રાજ્પૂતો ભેગા થયા હતા.

In Samvat 1296, on the 10th day of Magshar Sud, 778 years ago, a grand gathering of the Rajput community was held at Sidhpur. Thirty-six clans (Shakhas) came together at this historic meeting.

Twelve of these Shakhas, based on their standing and land holdings, came together to form a unified organisation — the Barsakh Rajput Kul. From that day all social dealings, matrimonial alliances and genealogical records were shared between these twelve clans.

Kheta Bhai sut Bhimji of Ankleshwar was appointed as the Barot (Ankleswariya), establishing the tradition of maintaining community genealogical records — a tradition that continues to this day.

Timeline

Key Milestones

Samvat 1296

Grand Gathering at Sidhpur

Thirty-six Rajput clans gather at Sidhpur. Twelve Shakhas unite to form the Barsakh Rajput Kul, establishing lasting social customs and alliances.

Tradition Est.

Barot Tradition Established

Ankleswariya Barot family appointed as official genealogy keepers for the community, preserving Vanshavali records across generations.

Ahmedabad

Community Settles in Ahmedabad

Barsakh Rajput families establish a strong presence in Ahmedabad, forming a vibrant urban community centre that continues to grow.

Gnati Panch

Formation of Gnati Panch

The formal Gnati Panch committee is established to govern community affairs, resolve disputes and organise cultural events.

Annual Events

Diwali Function Tradition

The annual Diwali function recognising academic, sports and community achievements becomes a cherished and well-attended tradition.

Digital Age

ambaraj.com Launched

The community launches its official website, bringing the Barsakh Rajput community into the digital era with online records and information.
